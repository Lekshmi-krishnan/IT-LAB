The College of Engineering and Management, Punnapra is an educational institution established in 2008 under the Co-operative Academy of Professional Education. The college is affiliated to Kerala University (till 2015) and APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) (from 2015) and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

The institution currently holds the distinction of having one of the largest engineering workshops in Kerala. It is fully controlled and funded by the Government of Kerala.
